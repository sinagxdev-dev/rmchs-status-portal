Announcements

Latest news and updates from the RMCHS Admissions Office

May 19, 2026
Admission Application for SY 2026-2027 is Now Open
Applications for incoming Grade 7 students are now being accepted for School Year 2026-2027. Applicants must submit all required documents through the online application form. The application period will run until slots are filled. Early submission is highly encouraged.
May 19, 2026
Examination Schedule Announced
Written examinations for STE and Regular applicants are scheduled on June 21, 2026. STE examinees are assigned to Room 101 while Regular examinees will be in Rooms 102 and 103. SPA skill tests will be conducted separately on June 22, 2026 in their respective venues.
May 19, 2026
Requirements Reminder
All applicants must submit a PSA Birth Certificate, Transcript of Records (Form 138), and Good Moral Certificate. Documents must be clear and readable. Blurry or incomplete submissions will be marked as Incomplete and applicants will be asked to resubmit.
May 19, 2026
Grade Requirements per Program
STE applicants must have a minimum grade of 90 in English, Mathematics, and Science. Regular applicants must have a minimum grade of 80 in all subjects. SPA applicants must pass the face-to-face skill test in their chosen specialization (Dance, Music, or Media Arts).
May 19, 2026
Status Portal Now Available
Applicants can now check their real-time application status through this portal. Simply enter your Applicant ID and registered email address on the Home page. Your Applicant ID was sent to your email upon submission of your application.

Programs Offered

Junior High School programs available for SY 2026-2027

Academic Track

College preparatory programs with specialized curriculum
STE

Science, Technology and Engineering

Designed for learners passionate about science, technology, and engineering through scientific inquiry and mathematical theory.

Min. 90 in English, Math, Science
Regular

Regular Junior High School

The standard Junior High School program following the K-12 curriculum with a well-rounded academic foundation.

Min. 80 in all subjects

Special Programs

Specialized tracks requiring a skill test and audition
SPA — Dance

Special Program in the Arts

For students passionate about interpretative and modern dance. Prepare one music piece for the skill test.

Requirements + F2F Skill Test
SPA — Music

Special Program in the Arts

For students with a passion for music. Prepare one song (Tagalog or English). You may bring your own instrument.

Requirements + F2F Skill Test
SPA — Media Arts

Special Program in the Arts

For aspiring content creators and digital artists. Prepare a sample edited photo, video, or graphic design.

Requirements + F2F Skill Test
